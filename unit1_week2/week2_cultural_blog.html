What are my important values?

March 23, 2014

When I think of my happiest moments, most of them are associated with FREEDOM and INDEPENDENCE. Some of them CLOSE RELATIONSHIP. And I guess one important one is HAVING GOOD FOOD. Definitely essential.
I think the reason why I consider Independence and Freedom very important lies much in my education from my mother. She is the person who emphasizes the idea of independence, being a female engineer in Japan, which makes her categorized in minorities. She struggles with her environment but never gives up, which spirit has affected me a lot.
Also my education in an all-girls school makes me more independence-focused as well. Teachers told me that even if I was a female, nobody would help me out so I had to be competent, which idea I've taken for granted.

In general, I try to live up to Independence---------5
In general, I try to live up to Freedom--------------5
In general, I try to live up to Close relationship---4
In general, I try to live up to Having good food-----3

The last topic that my friend asked for my advice on was Independence.
